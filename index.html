About Me

Hi, I'm Relieyan! I'm from Bali, Indonesia. I am a final year student at Bandung Institute of Technology with a major in Information System and Technology. I deeply passionate in Backend & Cloud Computing because I love to learn about how the system works and how to make it better. After many years of learning, I am now ready to take the next step in my career. I am looking for a position where I can use my skills and knowledge to help the company grow and achieve its goals. Beside of that, I want to keep learning, growing, be more experienced to be a better engineer.

Back to 2019, I started my college education at Bandung Institute of Technology. I've studied a broad of subjects, from technology, system, and management. With many years I've explored the broad of subject, in the end I decide to pick Backend & Cloud Computing to get deeper knowledge skills. Outside of my class, I take some of the courses and make some projects to improve my skills and knowledge.

All of the projects that I've learned until now, both in tech and non-tech, I put it in this website with repository link and some preview of the projects. if you interested to connect and collaborate together with me, can directly view my profile at the contact section. That's a briefly introduction about me, Thank you!
